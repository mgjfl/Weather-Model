class Data.data_generation.GaussianData(d: int, w: int)¶

Bases: Data.data_models.RandomSampler

A general class capable of modelling multivariate Gaussian distributions with time-dependent mean and covariance function.

The class can sample new datapoints through: 1. parameter_init which calls mean_init and covariance_init 2. sample

The loss is dependent on the log-likelihood of the model, given by log_probs
